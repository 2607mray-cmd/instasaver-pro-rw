## Step 1 ‚Äì Request Your Data from Instagram

You can do this from the Instagram app:

1. Open Instagram and go to your **Profile**
2. Tap **‚ò∞ Menu** ‚Üí **Settings & Privacy**
3. Go to **Your information and permissions**
4. Tap **Download your information**
5. Choose:

## Step 2 ‚Äì Extract Your Chat History

1. Download the ZIP file to your phone or computer
2. Extract/unzip the file
3. Look for folders like:
   - `messages/`
   - `direct/` or similar
4. Inside, you'll see multiple **JSON files** ‚Äì these contain your DM history
5. Each JSON file usually represents a conversation or set of messages

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long does Instagram take to send my data?**
A: Usually 24-48 hours, but can take up to 14 days for large accounts.

**Q: Is the data download free?**
A: Yes, Instagram's official data download is completely free.

**Q: Can I download someone else's chat history?**
A: No. You can only download your own data. Any tool claiming to download others' chats is likely a scam.

**Q: What format is the chat history in?**
A: JSON format by default. You can convert it to CSV/Excel for easier reading.

**Q: Will Instagram notify the other person if I download our chat?**
A: No. Downloading your own data is private and doesn't notify anyone.
